# iSnap-Review
Related data files and analysis pipeline for iSnap journal studies.



## Referenced Studies
The studies referenced within the repository originate from the following sources:

1. Price, T., Dong, T., & Barnes, T. (2016).
   Generating data-driven hints for open-ended programming.
   *Proceedings of the International Conference on Educational Data Mining (EDM)*.

2. Zhi, R., & Barnes, T. (2017).
   Hint generation under uncertainty: The effect of hint quality on help-seeking behavior.
   *Proceedings of the International Conference on Artificial Intelligence in Education (AIED)*.

3. Price, T., Zhi, R., Dong, Y., Lytle, N., & Barnes, T. (2018).
   The impact of data quantity and source on the quality of data-driven hints for programming.
   *Proceedings of the International Conference on Artificial Intelligence in Education (AIED)*.
